# Pass For Now — Support

_Last updated: 2026-04-21_

The fastest way to get help with Pass For Now is to email
**support@articraftor.com**. A human replies, usually within two
business days.

If you prefer not to switch to your mail client, the in-app
**Settings → Send Feedback** screen reaches the same inbox and
lets you include context without leaving the app.

---

## Common questions

### How do I cancel my subscription?

On your iPhone: **Settings** → tap your name at the top →
**Subscriptions** → **Pass For Now** → **Cancel Subscription**.
Cancelling takes effect at the end of your current billing period;
you keep premium access until then.

You can also reach this screen from inside the app:
**Settings → Manage subscription**.

### How do I restore a purchase?

Open the app → **Settings → Restore Purchases**. This works when
you're signed into the same Apple ID you originally purchased with.

### What happened to my data on my new phone?

Pass For Now stores your saves and profile locally on your device.
If you set up the new iPhone from an **iCloud backup** of the old
one, your app data restores with the backup. If you set up as a
new device, the old data stays on the old phone only.

### How do I delete my data?

Open the app → **Settings → Reset Data**. This wipes every local
record the app holds about you. Uninstalling the app also deletes
all of it.

To delete your subscription record at RevenueCat (our subscription
provider), email **support@articraftor.com** and we'll forward the
request.

### I'm not getting daily reminders.

Two places to check:

1. Inside the app: **Settings → Daily reminder** is on.
2. On iOS: **Settings → Notifications → Pass For Now** allows
   notifications.

Reminders fire at the time you set during onboarding. You can
change that time in **Settings → Daily reminder**.

### The app thinks I'm not Premium, but I paid.

Tap **Settings → Restore Purchases** first — that resolves most
cases. If it doesn't, email **support@articraftor.com** with the
Apple receipt email you received at the time of purchase and we'll
sort it out.

### I have a question that isn't here.

Email **support@articraftor.com**. No form, no ticket number, no
bot. A person will reply.
